Realm of Racket surely deserves the unique mantra it totes: "By freshmen, for freshmen". The book, inspired by Conrad Barski's Land of Lisp, follows a young man named Chad as he searches for the meaning of life and programming. He finds it in Racket, the most unique programming language in the world.

By:

Forrest Bice, Rose DeMaio, Spencer Florence, Mimi Lin
Scott Lindeman, Nicole Nussbaum, Eric Peterson, Ryan Plessner
